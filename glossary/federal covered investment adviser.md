# Definition
(sometimes just referred to as a "covered adviser")

> Typically refers to larger firms that register with the SEC:
    - they meet the minimum threshold of $100M (eligible to register with the SEC) or $110M in AUM (when SEC registration is required)
    - those under contract to manage an Investment Company under [[Investment Company Act of 1940]]
    - those registered with the SEC because they are excluded from the definition of an IA within the [[Investment Advisers Act of 1940]] - usually whose advice is limited to securities issued by the U.S. government or one of its agencies
